BrightAct is a benefit corporation based in Sweden. We partner with organizations, cities and authorities that inspire. Specifically, we enable collaboration accross borders using thoughtful tech to inovate how the public sector provides services to inhabitants. We believe in people over profits- thus commit to limit and reinvest profit to create impact where most needed.
