BrightAct

We advocate for equality with thoughtful tech to address domestic violence globally

BrightAct is a benefit corporation based in Sweden. We partner with organizations, cities, and authorities that inspire. Specifically, we enable collaboration across borders using thoughtful tech to innovate how the public sector provides services to inhabitants. We believe in people over profits - thus commit to limit and reinvest profit to create impact where most needed.
